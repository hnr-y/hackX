8.26-8.27

About

HackX is a hackathon hosted at Los Gatos Highschool where hundreds of students from across California will attend. HackX is a two day in-person event on the weekend of June 10th. If you're looking for an opportunity to flex your tech skills, collaborate with other innovative minds, and work on exciting projects, then you've come to the right place. Over the course of this hackathon, you'll have the chance to build something awesome from scratch, network with industry professionals, and compete for some amazing prizes. But HackX isn't just about the competition - it's also about the community. We believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute, whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out. That's why we've designed this event to be inclusive and supportive, with plenty of opportunities to learn, grow, and connect. So if you're ready to dive into the world of hackathons and see what you're capable of, then join us for HackX!

Dinner with leaders in tech, Paid Internship Opportunities and $10,000+ of Prizes

Prizes

On the final day of the hackathon, participants will have the opportunity to be recognized for their outstanding contributions and hard work. The top five winning teams will receive exciting prizes that include a paid internship at a leading tech firm, dinner with experienced mentors, a custom-built PC with an Nvidia 4070, a high-quality webcam and microphone, a one-year Spotify subscription and a mechanical keyboard. It is worth noting that all participants will receive a commemorative t-shirt, water bottle, and sticker to mark their participation in this memorable event.

Code

Industry experts from Google, Microsoft, and Nvidia will be present at our hackathon, offering participants the chance to receive feedback, collaborate, and gain insights into the tech industry. Workshops, mentoring sessions, and networking events will allow for meaningful interactions between students and experts, benefiting both parties. We're excited to provide this opportunity to our participants and look forward to the positive impact it will have.

Activities

Our hackathon will feature a Minecraft server, a Clash Royale tournament, and a scavenger hunt to promote collaboration, creativity, and strategic thinking among participants. These activities will provide a fun and engaging break from coding while fostering a positive atmosphere for all attendees. We encourage everyone to participate and make the most of these exciting opportunities.

Speakers

  • Pradeep Gupta

    Senior Director @ Nvidia

    Pradeep Gupta is the senior director of the Solutions Architecture and Engineering team at NVIDIA, where he is responsible for autonomous driving. His focus is on building production-grade AI that can be deployed in life-critical systems. Previously, Pradeep worked in areas like high-performance computing, computer vision, mathematical library development, and data center technologies. He received a master's degree in research from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. His research focused on developing compute-efficient algorithms.
