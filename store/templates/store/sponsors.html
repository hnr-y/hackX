Mission Statement

We inspire programmers of all skill levels to fall in love with technology and programming; our past six events have served over 2000 Bay Area high schoolers, giving them the opportunity to learn and apply new skills through the projects they make at our event.

Our hackathons have consistently been the largest highschool hackathon on the West Coast, and for several of our events, one of the largest highschool hackathons globally. This year, we are aiming to have 350 attendees, with an emphasis placed on including minority groups in STEM.

Since we were founded, we have made it our goal to maintain a 1:1 gender ratio and ensure that students, regardless of their background, can have the opportunity to attend our hackathon and become inspired to pursue a career in STEM.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Hackathon?

Hackathons are fast-paced events where individuals with diverse backgrounds collaborate in teams to create software or design solutions to real-world problems within a short timeframe, typically ranging from a few hours to a few days. The focus is on creativity, innovation, and producing a tangible result by the end of the event.

Is the Hackathon Free?

HackX is completely free for all participants, offering an opportunity for individuals to showcase their skills and connect with like-minded individuals in the tech industry.

What are the prizes?

First: Paid Internship at sponsor, iPad 10th generation, Custom built PC with Nvidia 4070 and Dinner with mentors
Second: Dinner with mentors
Third: Dinner with mentors
Forth: Logitech C920x Webcam and microphone
Fifth: One year Spotify subscription and mechanical keyboard
Everyone: HackX Branded T-shirt, water bottle and stickers

Is there food provided?

Yes, breakfast, lunch and dinner will be provided and free of charge. Please let us know of any accommodations you would like to have.

What is the time, date and location of HackX?

The first day is fully online which is on June 10th, the second day will be in-person on June 11th at Los Gatos Highschool in the library. HackX for both days will last from 8AM to 10PM.

When is the application deadline?

The application deadline is 5/22, make sure to sign up before then!

How do the teams work?

We will have teams of three who work together as a group on one project. Everyone of all skill levels can join!

What if I don't have a team?

Don't worry! We'll have team formation events at the beginning of the weekend geared towards helping you find people to work with.
